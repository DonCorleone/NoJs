01
overflow-y: scroll
The container must be a real scroller — a plain tall div won't create a timeline.
02
scroll-timeline-name
Names the scroller so deeply nested children can reach it — needed because overflow: hidden on .sticky-viewport would intercept scroll(nearest).
03
position: sticky
Pins the card row while 400 vh of content scrolls past behind it.
04
width: max-content
Lets the track grow as wide as all cards — without it, translateX has nowhere to go.
05
animation-timeline
Scroll down = move left. 1-to-1, no timers, no JavaScript.
06
calc(-100% + 100vw)
Moves the track left by its full width, minus one viewport — last card stops flush at the edge.
